05 — Recent Deals & Agreements

Four headline partnerships
of the last few years.

The four biggest, most recent agreements Teyseer Group has entered or announced — each linked back to the publication in which it was reported.

2024
May
Banking · Teyseer Group

MoU signed with Dukhan Bank — integrated banking, technology and SME financing.

Teyseer Group signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Dukhan Bank to explore joint propositions for startups and SMEs, including bundled offerings combining Dukhan Bank's SME banking with Teyseer Group's technology, plus BNPL and POS financing for SME capital expenditure (subject to regulatory approvals).

From: CB Insights · Dukhan Bank press release on Teyseer Group MoU
2024
January
Mobility · Teyseer Motors

Strategic partnership signed with Great Wall Motor (GWM).

Teyseer Motors signed a strategic partnership with GWM on 24 January 2024 — covering the GWM Tank premium SUV brand, the Haval SUV range, and the Poer / Wingle pickup line. A new showroom on Old Airport Road, Doha, was announced as part of the agreement.

From: Qatar Tribune · “GWM, Teyseer Motors transform Qatar's automotive landscape in new partnership” · 29 January 2024
2023
March
Technology · Computer Arabia

Computer Arabia — The 2022 Partner Award of the Year.

Computer Arabia, the Group's IT-cluster integrator, was recognised as “The 2022 Partner Award of the Year” — announced via Teyseer Group's news channel in March 2023.

From: teyseergroup.com / News · 29 March 2023
2022
July
HVAC · Teyseer AC & Refrigeration

Westinghouse HVAC partnership announced for Qatar.

Westinghouse Air Conditioning and Electronics Middle East confirmed it would supply VRF, ducted splits, rooftop units, applied solutions and the latest HVAC technologies through a partnership with Teyseer Group — announced via EINPresswire on 14 July 2022.
