02 — The Message

A message from
the Chairman.

A short reflection on where we started, where we are today, and where Teyseer Group is going next.

To
Our partners, our people, & the people of Qatar

When my father, the late Mr. Abdulrahman Essa Al Mannai, founded Teyseer Group in 1968, he envisioned a company built on service — believing national progress begins by enabling others to thrive. The name Teyseer, meaning "to facilitate," has guided us ever since, and it remains the intent we extend to every partner, customer and colleague who works with the Group today.

From those humble beginnings, Teyseer Group has grown into a multi-sector enterprise of 18 subsidiaries with between 5,000 and 10,000 employees — alongside the international partners and principals who have grown with us. Our work now spans mobility, infrastructure, technology, energy, security and services, supporting both Qatar's daily rhythm and its long-term ambitions, and the relationships at the heart of that growth are still our most important measure of success.

As we align with the goals of Qatar National Vision 2030, we are modernising operations, investing in our people and extending the trust we have built with our partners into future-ready sectors — always anchored in our founding values. The next chapter of Qatar's economy is being written now, and Teyseer's task is to meet it the way we have met every other: with patience, discipline and a deep respect for the people we serve.

From
Khalifa A.R. Al Mannai
Chairman · Teyseer Group
03 — Founder Tribute

A tribute.

A salute to our Founder Chairman, on whose standards the Group continues to build.

[Founder portrait · placeholder]
A Tribute · Founder Chairman

Mr. Abdul Rahman Essa Al Mannai — founder of the Group, 1968.

Our Founder Chairman, Mr. Abdul Rahman Essa Al Mannai, opened the first Teyseer office in Doha in 1968 — at a moment when Qatar was beginning a generational transformation. He believed that a Qatari business could earn the trust of international principals on the strength of its standards, and that a workplace built around long careers was the surest path to building those standards.

His belief in ethics, partnership and the long view is the foundation on which the Group still stands. Every company under the Teyseer name is a continuation of the standards he set — carried forward with pride.
