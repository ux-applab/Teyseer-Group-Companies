Automotive

Where Teyseer began — and where we continue to lead Qatar's mobility since 1968.

The Vertical
The foundation of the Teyseer story.

Teyseer's journey began in 1968 with automotive products and accessories. More than five decades on, mobility remains at the heart of the Group — anchored by our role as exclusive distributors for some of the world's most respected marques, and as market leaders in vehicle care across Qatar.

The Automotive vertical delivers a complete ownership journey: new-vehicle sales, certified after-sales service, flexible rental and leasing, and premium car care — all underpinned by the trust and quality that define the Teyseer name.

Heritage

Founded in 1968, Teyseer Group has been driving Qatar forward through trusted partnerships and exceptional automotive service for over 50 years.

Mission

To be the most preferred automotive partner in Qatar — delivering innovative solutions through friendly, efficient and factory-trained staff.

Vision

To be Qatar's most trusted partner in every automotive segment we serve — now and in the future.
