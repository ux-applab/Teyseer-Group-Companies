02 — Alignment

What ESG means
to Teyseer.

A statement of intent — how the Group thinks about environmental stewardship, social responsibility and governance discipline as one continuous commitment.

E

Environmental

Teyseer's environmental commitment is engineered into operations rather than declared on the side. Through our Farizon commercial EV representation and ongoing modernisation of the Scania transport fleet, we reduce emissions in the mobility of the country we serve. ExxonMobil lubricants since 1969 support cleaner engine performance, and our Microprecision Laboratories calibration joint venture improves measurement accuracy — lowering the day-to-day environmental impact of our industrial work.

S

Social

Teyseer is a family business, and the same care we extend to our 5,000–10,000 multinational colleagues is the care we extend to our partners, the local SMEs we work alongside, and the Qatari startups our Dukhan Bank MoU is built to support. We invest in local employment, training and career progression across automotive, industrial and services, and we measure our social contribution by the longevity of the relationships we keep.

G

Governance

Governance, for Teyseer, is the operational discipline that keeps every commitment honest. Our industrial operation (TISSCO) is ISO 9001-certified, our regulated services run under Ministry of Interior licensing, and the SAP S/4HANA Private Cloud digital backbone at Teyseer Motors makes our dealership operations auditable and data-driven. The whole framework rests on more than five decades of continuous Qatari ownership and ethical business practice.
