About Teyseer Holding.

Teyseer Holding is a progressive conglomerate of pioneering companies, driven by an entrepreneurial tradition of business integrity, goal-orientation and a spirit of service that has spanned over five decades. We are transformers of ideas with a strong penchant for innovation — courageous in our thinking, and responsible for the clients who put their trust in us.

Since 1968, we have built one milestone after another, fostering organizational values and a culture focused on acquiring relevant expertise. Today, Teyseer Holding has 18 independent entities working side by side and synergistically — promoting a healthy culture of the synergy of one for the power of all.

Our success is derived from a dynamic workforce of between 5,000 and 10,000 employees and the steadfast support of senior management who deliver solutions through periods of change, stability and growth.
