Appendix

Inner Pages — Design BenchmarksIndustry Verticals · Automotive · IT Details

A — Industry Verticals

A.1

Stats Strip — Aramco "At a Glance"

aramco.com →

The At a Glance stats band in the Industry Verticals and IT Details pages was benchmarked from Aramco's minimal large-number stats — bold numbers with short label and horizontal rule separator. Applied to Teyseer's 4-column strip across all vertical detail pages.

Used in: Industry Verticals Listing v2, IT Details Page

A.2

Key Achievements — Al-Futtaim News & Insights

alfuttaim.com →

The Key Accomplishments section was benchmarked from Al-Futtaim's News & Insights editorial card grid. Each card shows a category tag, date, bold headline, and source attribution — with a NE-arrow link to the original article. The hover-enlarge interaction and verifiable link pattern builds credibility for B2B audiences.

Used in: Automotive Details v2, Automotive Details v3, IT Details Page

B — Automotive

B.1

Business Units — GE Aerospace Brand Portfolio

geaerospace.com →

The Business Units / Our Companies section was benchmarked from GE Aerospace's brand portfolio: a static left column with heading and navigation arrows paired with horizontally scrollable tall image cards. Each card bears a company name, tagline, and arrow CTA. Teyseer adapts this for its three automotive companies — Teyseer Motors, TSC, Rent A Car.

Used in: Automotive Details v1, Automotive Details v3

B.2

Hero — Stripe Typographic Hero + Brand Marquee

stripe.com →

The Automotive v2 hero was benchmarked from Stripe's homepage: a bold oversized headline commanding the full viewport width, paired with a live-data stat line and a scrolling strip of trusted partner logos below CTAs. This pattern informed the large-headline approach, the stats strip, and the brand marquee in Teyseer Automotive v2.

Used in: Automotive Details v2

B.3

Highlights in Automotive — Al-Futtaim

alfuttaim.com →

The Key Brands & Projects section in Automotive v3 and IT Details was benchmarked from Al-Futtaim's Automotive highlights layout — full-bleed image cards with bold overlaid headlines and a "+" expand icon. This pattern informed the image-led card treatment and hover-reveal interaction for Teyseer's brand and project cards.

Used in: Automotive Details v3, IT Details Page

B.4

Selected Brands — Al-Futtaim 2×4 Logo Grid

alfuttaim.com →

The Our Partners / Brands sections were benchmarked from Al-Futtaim's Selected Brands: a two-column header layout above a clean 4-column logo grid. The two-col header pattern informed the section title + descriptor layout, while the logo grid informed the brand partner display in Teyseer's IT Details and Automotive pages.

Used in: Automotive Details v3, IT Details Page

B.5

Showrooms & Locations — Mercedes-Benz Dealer Locator

mercedes-benz-mena.com →

The Showrooms & Stations section in Automotive v3 — a sidebar list with filter pills and an interactive map — was benchmarked from Mercedes-Benz MENA's Dealer Locator. The left-panel list + right map split layout creates a familiar mental model for users looking for a specific type of location.

Used in: Automotive Details v3

B.6

Company Selector — Al Ghurair Contact Tabs

alghurair.com →

The Showrooms & Stations horizontal scroll strip in Automotive v2 was benchmarked from Al Ghurair's contact company selector — pill-shaped company tabs activate a detail card showing address, phone, email, and a Get Directions CTA. Teyseer adapts this into a horizontal scrollable card strip per location, keeping the same information density in a more compact format for 8 locations.

Used in: Automotive Details v2

C — Information Technology

C.1

IT Accomplishments — Siemens Press Highlights

siemens.com →

The Key Accomplishments section in the IT Details Page was benchmarked from Siemens' press page — each card features a full-bleed image, a location/category tag, a bold headline, and a short body paragraph. Teyseer adapts this into 3-column cards with category tag, date, title, one-line description, source attribution, and a NE-arrow link to the original article.

Used in: IT Details Page

C.2

IT Services — Siemens Left Tab Nav + Content Panel

siemens.com →

The IT Services section tab panel was benchmarked from Siemens' technology page — a vertical list of service topics on the left activates a centre image and a right content card. This 3-column panel structure enables exploration of many service categories (9 in Teyseer's IT vertical) without navigating away, essential for enterprise B2B audiences comparing solutions.

Used in: IT Details Page, Automotive Details v3 (Services section)
