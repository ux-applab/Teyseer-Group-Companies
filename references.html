Industry Verticals — Version 1 Open Page

The Industry Verticals v1 page presents a high-level overview of all 9 of Teyseer Group's business verticals, with stats, a sector navigator, key achievements, and a partner brands strip.

Section
Hero & Group Overview
Users arriving at the group verticals page need immediate orientation — who is Teyseer, how large are they, and what sectors do they cover? The hero anchors the page with a clear identity statement and a stats strip that communicates scale in seconds, reducing cognitive load before users scroll into detail.
Official Website
Teyseer Group corporate website — Group overview, founding year (1968), headquarters (Doha, Qatar), and business scope across 9 verticals. Primary source for the hero headline, subtext, and the "Est. 1968" positioning.
teyseergroup.com
Section
Stats Strip (10+ Projects, 18+ Companies, 8+ Awards, 9 Verticals)
Quantitative stats are the fastest way to communicate organisational scale without requiring users to read. The 4-stat strip is placed immediately below the hero fold so that users see credibility signals before engaging with individual verticals — anchoring trust early in the scroll journey.
Official Website
Teyseer Group — Group Companies — Source for the count of 18+ group companies and the 9 verticals. Each company page was reviewed to validate the company count.
teyseergroup.com
Official Brochure
Teyseer Group Official Corporate Brochure — Source for key project counts and award tally across the group. The brochure consolidates achievements that are not individually listed on the public website.
View Brochure
Section
Industry Verticals Panel (9 Sectors + Companies + Partner Brands)
The verticals panel is the primary navigation hub of the page. Each vertical expands to show its companies and partner brands — giving users a scannable map of the group's full scope without requiring separate page loads. Company chips link to official websites so users can validate any claim directly.
Official Website
Teyseer Group — All Group Companies — The complete list of companies under each vertical. Each company's name, sector classification, and official website URL was sourced here and cross-referenced with the brochure.
teyseergroup.com

Key Achievements Across the Verticals
Recent achievements build credibility and demonstrate the group's active momentum. Showing achievements labelled by vertical helps users connect abstract sector names to real outcomes — making the group tangible. Sourcing from verifiable news articles rather than marketing copy increases trust.
