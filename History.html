Before we were a group of companies, we were an idea: that national progress begins by enabling others to thrive. Our name has always reflected that purpose.

The name

Teyseer

/tay·seer/ · Arabic, noun
From the Arabic root — meaning to ease, to enable
Definition

“The name Teyseer means ‘to facilitate’ — chosen to reflect a company built on service, believing that national progress begins by enabling others to thrive.”

The name was chosen by our Founder Chairman when he envisioned a company built around enabling Qatar's progress. It has guided every decision the Group has taken since.

03 — Origin Story

A small office,
and a quiet conviction.

In 1968 — three years before Qatar's independence — a young Qatari businessman opened a modest trading office in Doha with one ambition: to facilitate what his country was just beginning to build.

Teyseer began with a small and humble beginning in 1968, originally trading in automotive products and accessories — the practical things a country on the cusp of rapid transformation would need to keep moving.

From that single line of business grew the conviction that has carried the Group through every decade since: that a company's purpose is to enable the people, projects and institutions around it. Over the ensuing years, the founder aligned the activities of the business very closely with the opportunities created by the developing State of Qatar — never far ahead of the country, never far behind it.

Where Qatar needed dependable vehicles, lubricants and aftermarket support, Teyseer became an automotive trading and service business. Where Qatar needed civil construction capability, materials and transport, the Group built those operations too. Each addition was a response to a specific national need rather than a corporate ambition — an approach that, over a half-century, would quietly produce eighteen specialist companies under a single Qatari ownership.

04 — Our Evolving Identity

Our Evolving
Identity

1968

Founding & Vision

Teyseer Group is established by the late Mr. Abdulrahman Essa Al Mannai with a clear mission: to support Qatar's development through essential services rooted in trust, integrity, and national responsibility.

1970–1980

Early Expansion

The Group enters trading, automotive distribution, and industrial supply, becoming a key enabler of Qatar's early economic transformation and infrastructure growth.

1990

Strategic Partnerships & Sector Growth

Teyseer forges alliances with global brands, expanding into new markets and broadening its verticals across high-impact sectors.

2000

Diversification & Consolidation

New subsidiaries are launched in security, infrastructure, and logistics, while the Group undergoes structural consolidation to strengthen governance and operational scalability.

2010

Digital & Organizational Transformation

Teyseer embarks on digital modernization, implements advanced governance frameworks, and invests in talent systems to position the Group for regional competitiveness.

2025

National Contributor & Multi-Sector Leader

Now comprising 18 subsidiaries with between 5,000 and 10,000 employees, Teyseer operates as a multi-sector national contributor, aligned with the priorities of Qatar National Vision 2030 and prepared for the future.
